What

We are building the first human programming language: a creative tool for people, not programmers. This language is open-ended like LEGO. Building blocks, not templates. And it's designed for mobile devices: the computers we all have.
It starts with a new kind of canvas. We call them cards. Not apps or websites, but cards. And anyone can make them—no coding required. Cards run natively on your phone, but they can link to each other and be embedded anywhere.

“Simple things should be simple. Complex things should be possible.”
–Alan Kay

The cards you make aren't floating in space. They exist in a network called Universe.
Universe isn't a single product or destination. It's a set of tools and a community designed to evolve what the world can do with the web.

Who

We are a group of designers & programmers, musicians & mathematicians, artists & philosophers.

"We are as gods and might as well get good at it. So far, remotely done power and glory—as via government, big business, formal education, church—has succeeded to the point where gross defects obscure actual gains. In response to this dilemma and to these gains a realm of intimate, personal power is developing—power of the individual to conduct his own education, find his own inspiration, shape his own environment, and share his adventure with whoever is interested."
–Stewart Brand

We come from companies like Google, Khan Academy, and Kickstarter. We have built startups before. We're students of thinkers old and new—Alan Kay, Seymour Papert, Doug Engelbart, Ted Nelson, Bill Atkinson, Stewart Brand, Bret Victor — and their creations—Hypercard, Smalltalk, Mindstorms, the Web.
Computers at their best decentralize power — they liberate the individual and facilitate collective intelligence. The devices have become mainstream but this profound freedom has not. We want to change that.
This is a dream project for each of us: a way to apply our learnings—and dreams—towards a better future for computing.
We're 7 people based in Brooklyn, NY. We've raised capital from premier investors, so we have the resources we need to make this dream real. Now we need you.
