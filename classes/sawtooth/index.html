Sawtooth
is a coffee shop in Brooklyn, New York created with the idea of combining delicious coffee with fresh indie synth music. We stock a wide variety of coffee blends prepared in every way you can think of, sold by cup or by the ounce. Delivered on a weekly basis, Sawtooth also releases free digital mixtapes for you to enjoy with or without a steaming cup of joe, keeping your music taste fresh and your palette fresher.
